The Milwaukee Art Museum as an institution does not accept unsolicited artist submissions. The Museum cannot identify, authenticate, or appraise any works of art. The Museum cannot assist with conservation, preservation, or art handling. The Museum’s exhibition schedule is planned approximately three to five years in advance, and is carefully balanced to present our viewers with a varied artistic program. Therefore, we are not able to accommodate unsolicited submissions for exhibitions.
