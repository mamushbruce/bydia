THE GROUP'S HISTORY

1925

Papadopoulos family, a refugee family from Pontus, moved to Katerini Greece in 1925.

1946 - 1958

Right from 1946 when Savvas Papadopoulos the eldest took his first steps in the business market in Pieria, with a small local glazings store. He continuously upgraded his range until he managed to expand by importing and exporting construction materials since 1958.

1975

Almost thirty years later, in 1975, his sons Panagiotis and Georgios Papadopoulos started their own enterprise of glass processing and trading in Thessaloniki in a westside neighborhood.

1982

In 1982, they were the first double insulating glass manufacturers. Within a decade the store was moved in Sindos in the Industrial Area of Thessaloniki in a 1800m2 area while in 1995 the premises reached the 4000m2.

2008

A mutual passion for excellence and the desire to blend creativity and technologies laid the foundation on the third generation brand of the GROUP, PCA in 2008. After thorough consideration and search Savvas Papadopoulos had an intuition and the renowned skill in transforming glazing systems into a complete solution for every glass application. PCA expanded the GROUPS’ horizons into the international scenery.

2011

Establishment of the R&D department with the aim of designing architectural glass systems.

2012

Participation in our first exhibition in (Glasstec Düsseldorf). Since then, we have participated in approximately 80 exhibitions in countries such as Italy, Germany, the United States of America, France, Greece, the United Arab Emirates, etc.

2018

 Establishment of a new Showroom in Athens.

2021

Acquisition of a second CNC aluminum processing machine.

2023

Establishment of a new Showroom at our facilities in the Industrial Area of Sindos, Thessaloniki Greece.

2023

Establishment of a new Showroom in Budapest.
