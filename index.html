At Busoga Youth Development Initiative (BYDIA), we are committed to creating opportunities that uplift the youth of Busoga. BYDIA serves as a beacon of hope, providing the resources, mentorship, and programs needed to empower young individuals, enabling them to thrive and shape a brighter future for themselves and their communities.

BYDIA

Our headquarters are situated in Magwa area in Jinja and we are rapidly expanding thanks to your Help and our exceptional projects: Poultry, Livestock, Tech initiatives and Programming

From our inception, innovation has been at the core of our identity, driving our unwavering commitment to research and technological advancements. This relentless pursuit of excellence ensures that our products not only meet the highest quality standards but also endure the test of time.

Most of all, we attribute our success to our most valuable asset: our people.

Objective: To ensure the physical and mental well-being of youth in Busoga. Highlights:

- Mental health awareness campaigns and free counseling sessions.

- Free medical checkups and health camps in remote areas.

- Nutritional education and fitness workshops.

- Training workshops in music, dance, and visual arts.

- Introduction to digital platforms for promoting and monetizing creative work.

What BYDIA Wants!

To see a united and empowered youth leading a thriving Busoga region, where every young person has access to the tools and resources they need to realize their dreams and contribute to a sustainable future.

OUR VALUES

At BYDIA, our vision is A vibrant and self-sustaining Busoga region where youth are empowered to lead and innovate for the betterment of society.

01

Education and Skills Training

We believe education is the cornerstone of development. BYDIA runs various programs aimed at improving literacy, digital skills, and vocational expertise. Our focus areas include:
- Scholarship programs for underprivileged youth.
- Computer and digital literacy workshops.
- Tailored vocational training for market-driven skills.

02

Entrepreneurship and Economic Empowerment

Economic empowerment is essential for sustainable development. BYDIA supports budding entrepreneurs through:
- Business incubation and mentoring programs.
- Access to funding and microloans for small businesses.
- Training on financial literacy and business management.

03

Leadership and Mentorship

Empowered youth make empowered communities. BYDIA promotes leadership by:
- Hosting leadership training workshops.
- Connecting youth with mentors in various fields.
- Encouraging participation in local governance and policy discussions.

04

Health and Wellness Advocacy

A healthy community is a strong community. We advocate for better health through:
- Youth-focused health campaigns on HIV/AIDS, mental health, and hygiene.
- Hosting wellness workshops to promote physical and mental well-being.
